About Me

Growing up, I naturally gravitated toward fixing and repairing things. I taught myself how to troubleshoot and resolve a wide range of computer issues.

My goal is to help people resolve their technical issues. Using a computer can be frustrating - especially when you’re not sure what’s going wrong. With my experience, I’m able to quickly identify and solve problems, making the process easier and less stressful.

Simple Tips

  • Restart First: Many small computer issues are fixed just by restarting your device!
  • Keep your software updated: Updates can fix bugs and keep you secure.
  • Beware of suspicious emails: If you didn’t expect it, don’t click it.
  • Back up your stuff: Use an external drive or cloud service for photos and important files.
