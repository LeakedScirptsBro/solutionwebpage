Simple Tips

  • 1
    Restart FirstMany small computer issues are fixed just by restarting — it's the oldest trick for good reason!
  • 2
    Keep Software UpdatedUpdates aren't just new features — they patch security holes that bad actors actively look for.
  • 3
    Beware of Suspicious EmailsIf you didn't expect it, don't click it. When in doubt, call the company using a number you look up yourself.
  • 4
    Back Up Your StuffUse an external drive or cloud service for photos and documents. Hard drives can fail without warning.
